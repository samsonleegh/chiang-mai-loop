Practical Notes

🛵 Scooter Safety

All routes are flat — CM grid, San Kamphaeng road, Hwy 106, Pai valley. Mountain roads (Doi Suthep, Pai route) handled by bus/songthaew.

📄 IDP Required

Get International Driving Permit with motorcycle endorsement before leaving SG. Police checkpoints common. Photo existing scooter damage.

🌧️ May Weather

Start of rainy season — afternoon thunderstorms. Ride mornings, indoor activities afternoons. Fewer tourists, greener landscapes.

💰 Budget (~SGD 700)

Stay: 500–1,000 THB/night. Food: 300–500/day. Scooter: 200–300/day. Buses: 150–400/leg. Sky Temple taxi: ~2,000 THB RT.

🚌 Key Bus Info

GreenBus for Lampang (Arcade 3). Aya/Prempracha for Pai (Arcade 2) — book 1–2 days ahead. Blue songthaew to Lamphun from Iron Bridge, 20 THB.

🍜 Must-Eat

Khao soi everywhere. Sai ua at Chang Phuak. Longan noodles Lamphun. Aroy One Baht Lampang. Pai Walking Street. Ban Santichon Chinese food.
